Interesting Facts about chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are increadibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are reowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have exellent hearing abilities.

Camel

Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • Baby camels are born without humps. They are however able to run within hours of birth.

Teddy-bear

Interesting Facts about Bear

  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.
  • They have far superior navigation skills to humans; excellent memories; large brain to body ratio.
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.

Panda

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
  • An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Cicada

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus
  • Females may be attracted to the sound of motors
  • Most have red-orange eyes.
